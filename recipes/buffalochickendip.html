Zesty Buffalo Chicken Dip

Tangy, Zesty, Mouth-watering Buffalo Chicken Dip

Bits of succulant all white chicken smothered in zesty
buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, and smothered in cheesy
goodness, will leave you craving more of this flavorful dip!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken (canned or rotisserie)
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Cayenne Pepper Hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl and transfer to a 1-quart baking dish.

  2. Bake 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired,
    and serve with chips, crackers, and/or cut up veggies.
