Fiesta Street Corn

Juicy Corn Packed with Flavor!

Golden pieces of juicy corn topped
with

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chili crisp sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Meanwhile, trim the bottoms from Brussels sprouts and halve.

  2. Place Brussels in a large bowl and toss with olive oil, chili crisps and salt. Transfer to a
    rimmed baking sheet.

  3. Roast Brussels for 10 minutes or until desired doneness is reached. Enjoy!
